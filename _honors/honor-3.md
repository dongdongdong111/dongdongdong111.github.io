The Group Programming Ladder Tournament (GPLT), also known as the "TiTianSai," is a distinctive and large-scale programming competition in China, primarily targeted at undergraduate students. It emphasizes individual coding ability within a team framework and aims to elevate the fundamental programming skills of a broad student population.
